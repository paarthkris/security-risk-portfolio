# 02 - Risk And Controls

This section turns HarborPay's business context into prioritized security risk scenarios. It also shows which NIST CSF 2.0 categories and CIS Controls v8.1 areas would help address those risks.

## Files

- [Risk register](risk-register.md): scoring method, ten risk scenarios, owners, treatments, and evidence requests.
- [Control mapping](control-mapping.md): learning-oriented mapping from HarborPay risks to control objectives and test evidence.
- [Executive summary](executive-summary.md): a concise business-level view of the assessment.

## Status

Documentation complete for the fictional scenario. The ratings are inherent-risk estimates based on stated assumptions. They are not findings from a real audit, and I do not assign residual risk until a control has been tested.
